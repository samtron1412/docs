# vestigial

## adjective

- Forming a very small remnant of something that was once much larger or
  more noticeable.
    + thuoc ve dau tich
- Sample sentences
    + He felt a vestigial flicker of anger from last night.
    + We need to clean all of our vestigial resources which are part of
      the larger pipeline.
